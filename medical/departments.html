Anaesthesia

Welcome to the Department of Anaesthesiology, Pain medicine and Intensive Care at The Medicare Hospital. The department is committed to place the interests of the patients as its paramount concern. Our work is just not confined to the state of art-ultra modern 5 Operation Theatres (OTs), but also beyond it. It extends from the Pre-anaesthesia clinics (PAC), intra-operative care, Post Anaesthesia Care Units (PACU) and 20 bedded Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU) where patients are closely monitored by our team. We provide clinical anaesthesia services and critical care management of surgical patients 24 hours a day. The surgical specialities to which the department provides anaesthesia services are general surgery, orthopaedics, obstetric and gynaecology, E.N.T., paediatric surgery, urology, gastroenterology, cardiac surgery, onco-surgery and neurosurgery. Our mission is to deliver excellent anaesthesia services, pain management and intensive care for the patient.

  • Epidural Analgesia
  • Intensive Care
  • General Anaesthesia
  • Spinal Anaesthesia
  • Nerve blocks for chronic pain
  • Multimodal Anaesthesia

Cardiology

The department of Cardiac Care includes cardiology and cardiac surgery. It represents one of the largest and most modern cardiac care facilities which are affordable and available to one and all. Our dedicated team of cardiologists and cardiac surgeons are well-known for their expertise, knowledge, and vast experience in their respective arena of heart related problems. The Medicare Hospital provides a comprehensive range of diagnostics as well as therapeutic services for patients with Cardio Vascular ailments. The Centre provides dedicated round the clock services to all patients with heart ailments,havingthe best response in acute heart attacks for its effective practices to treat heart attacks during the Golden Hour: first hour after onset of a heart attack, during which if the person gets required treatment he can expect near-complete recovery. We also have the Cardio-Thoracic & Vascular Surgery division that is equipped with all modern facilities to conduct Coronary Bypass Surgery (CABG), valve replacement surgeries, aortic root and aneurysm surgeries. The department also has an array of non-invasive services like TransoesophagealEchocardiology, Echocardiography, Treadmill (including Stress Echo and Dobutamine Stress Echo Testing), 24 hour Holter monitoring, 24 hour Ambulatory blood pressure monitoring and Head up Tilt table testing. The advanced diagnostic services include CT Coronary Angiography as well as a range of Nuclear Cardiology services.

We specialize in Treating

  • Coronary Angiography
  • Periphery Angioplasty
  • Coronary Angioplasty/ Stenting
